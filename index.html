Hi, I'm Nina!

I was born and raised in Salt Lake City, and I graduated from the University of Utah with a BS in biology. Following graduation, I spent several years living and working in Japan before moving to Chicago. I’m passionate about both science and art, with an interest in facilitating dialogue between scientific and medical communities and the general public. Apart from my interests in biology and art, I'm an avid crossword-solver, birdwatcher, and cactus collector.
